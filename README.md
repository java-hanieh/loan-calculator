### Loan Payment Calculator  

Calculates monthly payments and total interest for a loan.  

**Description:**  
Takes the total loan amount, repayment period (in years), and annual interest rate as input.  
Uses the standard loan formula to compute:  
- Monthly payment  
- Total interest over the loan period  

**Example:**  
Input → Total Loan: 100, Period: 2 years, Interest: 18%  
Output →  
Each Payment: 4.98 Million Toman  
Total Interest: 19.5 Million Toman
